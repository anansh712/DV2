Australian Health Insights Dashboard (FIT3179)

Exploring national patterns in obesity, physical activity, BMI distribution, and nutrition (ABS & AIHW, 2022).

1) Obesity Rates by State

Choropleth of adults classified as obese (BMI ≥ 30). Darker shades indicate higher prevalence. Hover for exact values; small jurisdictions are labelled by abbreviation for clarity.

Source: ABS National Health Survey, 2022.

2) Physical Activity by Age

Grouped bars show the share of people reporting different days per week of activity. Use the dropdown to focus a single category. Ordering places 5 or more before 5–6 for consistency.

Source: ABS Physical Activity, 2022.

3) BMI Classification by Age  /  Sex

100% stacked bars compare Underweight, Normal, Overweight, and Obese within each age group, faceted by sex. Read across each row to compare composition.

Source: ABS Health Survey (Persons), 2022.

4) Fruit vs Vegetable Consumption

Dumbbell plot shows average daily fruit (blue) vs vegetable (green) serves by age group, with dotted guidelines at ~2 and ~5 serves. Labels quantify the fruit-to-veg gap.

Source: ABS Nutrition Survey, 2022.
